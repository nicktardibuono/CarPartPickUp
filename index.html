Car Part Pickup

  • High Concept: Race your red sports car to collect the parts as fast as possible.
  • Player Experience Goals: Racing against friends to set the fastest time.
  • Genre: Racing/Platformer
  • Game play: The player needs to collect the tires as fast as posssible using the arrow keys to move and up to jump. Collect all the tires then find the final part.
  • Prototype goal: Playing a top down platformer instead of left to right.
  • Features: The top down aspect and the late spwanig tire.
  • Setting/Premise: Your car lost some of its parts and you need to get the parts back to fix it. The more parts you get the faster your car goes. Get all the parts and your car starts.
  • Story: Nothing more than above.
  • Target Audience: Casual/Competive games.
  • Play time: Less than a minute.
  • Strategies: Dont stop moving and go fast.
